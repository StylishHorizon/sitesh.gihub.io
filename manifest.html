Manifesto

We don’t restore formats. We restore memory.

Stylish Horizon exists to preserve what matters — not what sells. MiniDV, VHS, Hi8, forgotten drives — each one holds fragments of truth. We treat them with care, not nostalgia.

No filters. No enhancement. No storytelling. Just clarity, context, and quiet respect.

We believe memory should be accessible, not monetized. Restoration is not a service — it’s a responsibility.
