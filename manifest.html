Restoration Services

We don’t sell nostalgia. We preserve it — with precision, honesty, and respect for the original signal. Below are our core restoration packages, designed for MiniDV and similar digital formats. No fluff. Just results.

Package 1: Legacy

  • For: Those who want to rescue aging footage and restore basic clarity.
  • Deinterlacing: Removes combing artifacts for smoother playback.
  • Basic Color Correction: Restores faded tones and balances exposure.
  • Noise Reduction: Cleans up digital grain and compression artifacts.
  • Output: Standard Definition (SD), optimized for archival and casual viewing.

Package 2: Ultimate Restoration

  • For: Clients seeking the highest fidelity for personal or professional use.
  • Includes all Legacy features, plus:
  • Advanced Color Grading: Cinematic tone mapping and contrast refinement.
  • Detail Enhancement: Sharpening and texture recovery without artificiality.
  • Artifact Removal: Frame-by-frame cleanup of glitches, dropouts, and scratches.
  • Upscaling: Intelligent conversion to Full HD (1080p) or optional 4K.
  • Output: High Definition (HD or 4K), suitable for modern displays and archival longevity.
